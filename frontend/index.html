Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples have a deep connection to the land and its seasonal rhythms. Unlike the four-season model, many Indigenous communities recognise multiple seasons based on local environmental cues. The Aboriginal seasonal calendars provide insights into weather patterns, animal behaviour, and environmental changes.
